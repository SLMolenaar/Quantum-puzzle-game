Quantum Maze

How to Play

Guide both players (red and blue) to their targets in the mazes. The players are quantum-entangled - moving one affects the other!

Complete each level by reaching both targets.

Press space to rotate the maze on the right.

The maze increases in size after each level.

Good luck and plan your moves carefully!
