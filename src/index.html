Quantum Maze

How to Play

Guide both players (red and blue) to their targets in the mazes. The players are quantum-entangled - moving one affects the other!

Complete each level and get points by reaching both targets. Watch out for walls as they can take away points.

To prevent hitting walls you can rotate the maze on the right side.

Good luck and plan your moves carefully!
